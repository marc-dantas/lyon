### Commands
- `mclear` [here](#mclear)
- `mwrite <value>` [here](#mwrite)

<hr>

<h3 id="mclear">Command: <code>mclear</code></h3> 

- Clears the `@SPACE` space in the memory.
- Syntax: `mclear`

<hr>

<h3 id="mwrite">Command: <code>mwrite</code></h3>

- Writes a value to the `@SPACE` space in the memory.
- Syntax: `write <value>`
